
Numerical Columns

    DUE_FREQUENCY – Payment frequency (weekly, monthly)
    NET_RENTAL – Amount of installment per period
    NO_OF_RENTAL – Number of installments agreed
    FINANCE_AMOUNT – Loan principal
    CUSTOMER_VALUATION – Value of the collateral/asset
    EFFECTIVE_RATE – Interest rate (%)
    AGE – Borrower’s age
    INCOME – Borrower’s income

Categorical Columns

    CONTRACT_STATUS – Target variable (Default vs Non-Default)
    MARITAL_STATUS_M – Married
    MARITAL_STATUS_Other – Unknown
    MARITAL_STATUS_S – Single
    MARITAL_STATUS_W – Widowed/Separated
